The content is a bit aged, and needs to be updated to fit a more modern Linux distribution. Using Fedora, RHEL, CentOS and similar distributions as a guide for user-friendliness is what the resulting presentation needs to have.

There needs to be informative and some humours images as part of the presentation. It also needs to show practical examples done in Linux - not just marketing/sales hype talk.

The goal is to take the "wow that's hard" out of the picture by showing a series of "easy button" solutions - that do not go too deep, but show that typical admin tasks aren't as problematic as some want to make them.
